About us

Founded in 2020, Chathubb has grown into one of the largest and most successful chat moderation companies globally. Join our team and become a part of our community!

At Chathubb, our chat moderators play a key role in energizing online communities by engaging in text-based conversations. They spark meaningful discussions, build lasting relationships, and create a fun, imaginative experience for users.

Building rapport and making customers feel valued, our moderators ensure positive, enjoyable interactions that keep users coming back and contribute to the overall success of our platform.

Important Information

If you are a U.S. contractor, freelancer, or consultant and you receive payments exceeding $600 from us in a tax year, you will be required to submit a completed W-9 form. This form provides your taxpayer identification information, which we use to report your earnings to the IRS on Form 1099-MISC.

Due to the freelance nature of this role and California's regulations regarding additional benefits for freelancers, we regret to inform you that we cannot accept applications from California residents.
