2. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device (computer, tablet, smartphone) when you visit a website. Cookies are widely used to make websites work more efficiently and to provide information to the website owners.

3. Types of Cookies We Use

We use the following types of cookies:

3.1 Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for the operation of our Services. They enable you to navigate our site and use its features, such as accessing secure areas.

3.2 Performance Cookies

These cookies collect information about how you use our Services, such as which pages you visit most often. This information helps us improve our Services and enhance your user experience.

3.3 Functionality Cookies

These cookies allow our Services to remember choices you make (such as your username, language, or the region you are in) and provide enhanced, more personalized features.

3.4 Targeting/Advertising Cookies

These cookies are used to deliver advertisements relevant to you and your interests. They may also limit the number of times you see an advertisement and help measure the effectiveness of advertising campaigns.

4. How We Use Cookies

We use cookies for the following purposes:

  • To provide and maintain our Services
  • To analyze usage and trends to improve our Services
  • To personalize your experience on our site
  • To show you relevant advertisements
  • To remember your preferences and settings

5. Managing Cookies

You have the right to decide whether to accept or reject cookies. You can manage your cookie preferences through your browser settings. Most web browsers allow you to refuse or accept cookies, and you can also set your browser to alert you when cookies are being sent. Please note that if you choose to refuse cookies, you may not be able to use the full functionality of our Services.
